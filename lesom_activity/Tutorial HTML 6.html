Roles In Jujutsu Kaisen

  1. Gojo
  2. Yuji
  3. Megumi
  4. Sukuna
  5. Kugisaki
  6. Yuta


Gojo is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. Sator is the pride of the Gojo Family, the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years.









Yuji is the son of Jin Itadori and Kaori Itadori. Yuji is the grandson of Wasuke Itadori who was living a normal life until he encountered Megumi and ate one of Sukuna's fingers.










Megumi is a grade 2 jujutsu sorcerer and first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High alongside Yuji Itadori and Nobara Kugisaki.Megumi was born outside the clan as Toji's trump card and was eventually found by Satoru Gojo, who mentored him and enrolled him at Jujutsu High as his student.










Sukuna is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer from a thousand years ago. Regarded as the undisputed King of Curses , Sukuna is one of the primary antagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series.







Kugisaki is a first-year student and grade 3 jujutsu sorcerer at Tokyo Jujutsu High studying under Satoru Gojo alongside Yuji and Megumi.







Yuta is initially a special grade cursed human haunted by his late childhood friend, Rika Orimoto. Satoru Gojo mentored Yuta and enrolled him at Tokyo Jujutsu High.
