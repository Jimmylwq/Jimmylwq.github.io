Simple Sponge Cake


Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g caster suger
  • 85g self-raising flour


Notes on Ingredients

Castor Sugar

Finely granulated white suger

Self-raising Flour

A pre-mixed combition of flour and leavening agents (usually salt and baking powder).


Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190.c
  2. Greare the oven 20 even round cake pan
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs ans caster sugar until fluffy
  4. Fold in flour
  5. Poor mixture into the prepared pan
  6. Bake 20 minites in the preheated oren until the top of the cake spring back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wirw rack
